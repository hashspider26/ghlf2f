TELEGRAM ONBOARDING BOT
Developer Specification Document
Project Owner: Suzie / Darius Benders  |  Date: April 9, 2026
1. Overview
Build a Telegram bot that monitors a closers group chat, parses sales reports posted by closers, and automatically fills in corresponding rows in a Google Sheet. The Google Sheet is already connected to Go High Level (GHL), so once the sheet is updated, data flows into GHL automatically.

The dev will also be given GHL admin access to configure tags/workflows directly.

2. System Architecture
The bot follows this flow:
Closer posts a sales report in the Telegram closers group.
The Telegram bot parses the message against the expected format.
If valid: Match the client in the Google Sheet, fill in payment columns, apply the correct GHL tag, and send a confirmation message.
If invalid (unexpected amount, bad format, etc.): Post an alert to the Internal VA Group asking the team to coordinate manually.

6. GHL Tag & Onboarding Workflow
The onboarding process uses GHL tags to trigger automations. The bot’s primary job is to apply the correct agreement tag when a closer reports a valid payment, then listen for downstream events to update the sheet and send confirmation messages.

Step 1: Deposit (Already Automated — No Bot Action)
Client pays $500 deposit. GHL auto-creates a row in the sheet and applies the deposit tag.
Tag: #c2c-deposit-april
Workflow: Enrollment Agreement (deposit payer)
Sheet update: Columns P and Q — handled by GHL

Step 2: Closer Reports Payment (BOT ACTION REQUIRED)
This is the bot’s primary trigger. When the closer posts a valid sales report, the bot must:
Match the client in the Google Sheet by full name AND/OR email.
Update the Google Sheet: Payment Plan, Payment Platform, Date, 1st Payment, Notes.
Apply the correct GHL tag (see table in Section 5).
Post a confirmation message to the Closers group AND the Internal VA Group:

    “This agreement has been sent.”

If the amount is NOT recognized, do NOT update the sheet or apply any tag. Instead, alert the Internal VA Group for human intervention.

Step 3: Client Signs Mentorship Contract (GHL Automation)
Once the client signs the mentorship agreement, GHL automation fires. The bot must listen for this event and check off column R (“Mentorship Contract Signed?”) in the sheet.

Step 4: Onboarding Course Access — Onboarding Tags
After signing, GHL applies the onboarding tag. The bot must listen for these tags, check off column S (“Mentorship Course Access”), and post a message to both groups:

    “The course has been sent to the mentee.”


7. Edge Case Handling
If the report does NOT match the expected format (unrecognized amount, missing fields, bad format), the bot should:
NOT update the Google Sheet or trigger any GHL tags.
Post a message to the Internal VA Group alerting the team.
Include the original message and ask the team to coordinate the sales report manually.
